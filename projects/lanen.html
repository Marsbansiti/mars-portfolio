In de
Lanen I

For the newest sub-area of Nieuw Kralingen, In de Lanen I, YUCONVR proudly provided the architectural impressions and animations. This special residential project sits in a unique location in Rotterdam, where city living, nature, and style seamlessly come together. In de Lanen I features 39 homes and 6 self-build plots, designed by four architectural firms that together provide a rich variety of stately mansions and characterful townhomes. The visualizations capture the characteristic Nieuw Kralingen architecture, with a strong focus on detail, atmosphere, and the power of the location.

The location is highly unique: the water of the canal runs right through the area, and both the Kralingse Bos and the Kralingse Plas are within walking distance. Residents will also enjoy spacious outdoor areas, as all homes in this plan feature both a city garden and a front garden. Through atmospheric images and animation, YUCONVR showcases the future of this new Rotterdam district, allowing future residents to feel what it will be like to come home here.
