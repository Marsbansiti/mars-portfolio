De Groene
Zoom I

Welcome to De Groene Zoom I, the newest residential ensemble within Nieuw Kralingen: a lively, green city district on the outskirts of Rotterdam. Here city and nature come together, with stately homes, deep gardens and the tranquility of the Kralingse Bos within walking distance. At YuconVR we are proud that we were allowed to take care of the visualisations and the animation for this special project. Commissioned by the developers, we brought De Groene Zoom I to life before the foundation stone was laid — with the aim of inspiring and helping future residents in making their choice.
