Indochine
Villa

Designed based on the distinct spirit of the homeowner, this District 7 villa by Kiến Phong Architects delivers a unique Indochine experience through the use of premium materials and exquisite detailing. The primary goal was to bring nature directly into the interior spaces. To achieve this, large windows and doors were integrated to welcome natural light and fresh breezes. A prominent side swimming pool not only offers a relaxing retreat but also serves to naturally cool the entire house. The architects dedicated significant time and passion to every individual space, resulting in a luxurious, elegant, and comfortable living environment that exceeds the client's expectations.
